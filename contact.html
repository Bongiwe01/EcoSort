Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the sorting information?

Our database is regularly updated with the latest recycling guidelines and waste management practices. We work with local waste management facilities to ensure accuracy.

Is EcoSort available in my area?

EcoSort provides general waste sorting guidelines that apply broadly. However, specific local regulations may vary. We recommend checking with your local waste management authority for area-specific rules.

Can I suggest new items to add?

Absolutely! We're always looking to expand our database. Contact us with items you'd like to see added, and we'll research and include them in future updates.

Is there a mobile app?

EcoSort is currently a web-based platform that works great on mobile browsers. A dedicated mobile app is in development and will be available soon!
